Campus closure — making a gift Dec. 23, 2021–Jan. 2, 2022

Many UC Santa Cruz offices will close 12/23–1/3. University Relations/UCSC Foundation maintains coverage during this time to help facilitate year-end giving. The easiest way to make a gift is through the online giving form.

Please make online gifts no later than 11:59 p.m. (PST) on December 31, 2021. For other gifts to be considered within tax year 2021, they must be postmarked by December 31, 2021.

For more information about making a gift, contact Gift Administration, (831) 332-5136.
