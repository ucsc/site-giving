UCSC Trustees

Downloads

  • Report on Philanthropy, with more stories of giving (pdf)

The UC Santa Cruz Foundation is a nonprofit, all-volunteer organization formed in 1974 to support the University of California, Santa Cruz. In close partnership with the Chancellor, faculty, and staff, the Foundation encourages and accepts private gifts to address critical campus needs.
